Guidelines and Emergency Support

Getting Started

How to use the Map?

Click on the map icon in navigation to see your live location and nearby safety zones marked with pink dots.

Safety Features

Emergency Alert (SOS)

The red alert button notifies nearby authorities and emergency contacts with your current GPS coordinates.

Quick Call

Use the phone icon to instantly dial local emergency services (112) or your pre-saved guardians.

Account Support

Profile Settings

You can update your personal information and contact details from the profile section at any time.
